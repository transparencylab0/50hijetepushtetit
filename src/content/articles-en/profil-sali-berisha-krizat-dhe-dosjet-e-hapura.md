This profile is not a judgment of guilt and it is not a list of accusations. It is a working map for a figure who has held high public power: president of Albania from 1992 to 1997, prime minister from 2005 to 2013 and a leading Democratic Party figure across several periods.

For this archive, the useful question is not whether a political figure is "for" or "against" one side. The useful question is simpler: **which decisions, crises, privatizations, investigations or sanctions connect to public office, and which documents should readers see?**

## Separate the periods

Berisha should be read across two different periods of power. As president, 1992-1997 connects to transition, weak institutions and the pyramid-scheme crisis. As prime minister, 2005-2013 connects to major public projects, state security, Gerdec, January 21 and decisions that now need to be read through documents.

That separation matters because a serious profile should not collapse everything into one sentence. Each topic needs its own file.

## Topics for future files

- **The 1997 crisis and pyramid schemes.** This should be followed through documents on financial regulation, government response, public warnings and decisions taken or not taken.
- **Gerdec.** The public questions concern the chain of institutional responsibility: contracts, authorizations, oversight, investigations, court decisions and the Defence Ministry's role at the time.
- **January 21, 2011.** Sources should separate proven facts from political accusations: who issued orders, what the investigation documented, which court decisions exist and why part of the investigation was resumed.
- **The Partizani / Malltezi case.** Public reporting describes corruption charges linked to property privatization and construction development. Berisha and the family members involved have denied the allegations and called them political.
- **U.S. and U.K. restrictions.** Here the text must distinguish an administrative designation from a criminal court finding. The restrictions are a public fact; their content and the objections to them should be quoted accurately.

## Editorial boundary

This profile should not be used to say "everything is true" or "everything is political." Both are propaganda shortcuts. The better format is slower: document, date, institution, mentioned party, right of reply.

When a claim is under investigation or trial, the text should say so clearly. When a party denies it, the denial should be noted. When the base document is missing, the question should remain open.

## What to request

- Government decisions and bylaws from the 1992-1997 and 2005-2013 periods.
- Court files, SPAK/GJKKO/Supreme Court decisions and international decisions where relevant.
- Contracts, privatizations, permits, memoranda, orders and minutes.
- Asset declarations and family links only when tied to a public document.
- Replies from Berisha, family members, institutions and other mentioned parties.

If those materials are missing, the profile remains an index of questions. Only once the documents are in hand should each topic become a full evidence file.
