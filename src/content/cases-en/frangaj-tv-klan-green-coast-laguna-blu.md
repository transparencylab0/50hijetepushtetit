## Names to follow

- **Aleksander Frangaj**: media owner and reported construction/property actor.
- **TV Klan / Frangaj family**: media chain to compare with public payments and permits.
- **Edi Rama**: Prime Minister; according to VOA, publicly defended the legality of the Laguna Blu paperwork.
- **Green Coast / Balfin / Samir Mane**: project context where each party's exact role must be clarified.
- **NTC / AKPT**: holders of the decision file, maps and conditions.

## What is known so far

Public sources present **Aleksander Frangaj** as an important media figure with expansion into construction and real estate. This does not prove favouritism. But when a media owner enters coastal projects, the public has a right to see permits, contractual role, payments and comparison with similar cases.

The file has two separate points: **Laguna Blu**, where the public conflict concerned permits and contested space; and **Green Coast**, where the official NTC decision and exact role of developers must be obtained.

## What cannot yet be said

It cannot be said that there is corruption. It cannot be said that the permit is illegal. It cannot be said that media was used as pressure. These questions are not closed by statements. They are closed by decisions, contracts, payments, maps and right of reply.
