## Names to follow

- **Bashkim, Astrit and Ahmet Ulaj**: figures reported in the Gener 2/A2 chain.
- **Gener 2**: construction group with concessions and public projects.
- **A2 CNN / G2 Media**: the media asset that adds a public-influence dimension.
- **DH Albania**: the company in the forged-documents scandal; alleged links must be proven.
- **ARRSH / Ministry of Infrastructure / prosecution**: holders of the key files.

## What is known so far

Public sources place **Gener 2** in several important contracts and concessions, including the debate over **Thumane-Kashar**. **A2** adds the media dimension: when a group receiving public contracts also has media, the public has a right to see state advertising, payments and possible conflicts.

The **DH Albania** file is the strongest verification point because it is not merely political debate: forged documents show a serious procurement-control problem. What is missing is proof of a real link, if any, to other actors.

## What cannot yet be said

It cannot be said that Gener 2 was behind DH Albania without documents. It cannot be said that A2 influenced public contracts. It cannot be said that the concessions are illegal. But the questions are of public interest and should be closed with files, not silence.
