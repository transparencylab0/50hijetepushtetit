- **Fabiola and Fatmir Hysenbelliu**: mentioned in the ownership chain according to MOM.

## What is known so far

Public sources report a clash in which **Hysenbelliu** presented state action as pressure against media, while the government presented it as law enforcement. Neither position can be verified without the administrative and court file.

The most important point is chronology: when the editorial line changed, when inspections arrived, what institutions said, what the court said and how similar cases were treated.

## What cannot yet be said

It cannot be said that the state retaliated without documents. It cannot be said that the building was legal without decisions. But if demolition happened before the court process closed, the public should see the file.
