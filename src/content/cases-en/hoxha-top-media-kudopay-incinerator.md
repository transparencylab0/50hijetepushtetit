## Names to follow

- **Hoxha family / Top Media**: media group with large audience.
- **Vjollca Hoxha**: reported shareholder in Kudopay according to MOM.
- **Stela Gugallja**: mentioned in the context of incinerators and Kudopay according to public sources.
- **AZHBR**: the agency where the proposal for promotional programmes is mentioned.
- **Edi Rama**: Prime Minister; made a public accusation against Top Channel about illegal favours according to MOM.

## What is known so far

Public sources report that **Vjollca Hoxha** had participation in **Kudopay** together with **Stela Gugallja**, a figure linked to the incinerator case. This does not prove money flows, editorial influence or benefit from the incinerators. Extracts, transactions and court files are needed.

The second point is the reported **AZHBR** proposal: 50 promotional programmes worth 12 million ALL. It must be seen whether this was a proposal, contract, payment or unresolved public conflict.

## What cannot yet be said

It cannot be said that Top Media benefited from incinerators. It cannot be said that Kudopay received suspicious money without documents. It cannot be said that the AZHBR proposal became payment. But the links are public enough to require documents.
