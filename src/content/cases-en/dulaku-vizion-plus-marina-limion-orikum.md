## Names to follow

- **Artan, Genci and Adrian Dulaku**: figures reported by MOM.
- **Concord Investment / Marina Limion / Marina Orikum / Marina Bay Saranda**: entities and projects for verification.
- **Kastrati Group**: reported partner in Limion.
- **Artur Mecollari**: former Navy official mentioned as complainant in reporting.
- **SIC / Council of Ministers / Ministry of Defence**: links holding acts, valuations and maps.

## What is known so far

Public sources place the **Dulaku** family at the intersection of media, construction and coastal projects. That is not proof of abuse. But when a project touches ports, marinas or former military property, the contract, term, tariff, asset valuation and public benefit must be visible.

The most sensitive point is the property chain: if an asset was military or public, the public should see what decision removed it from public use, what value it had and how the state interest was protected.

## What cannot yet be said

It cannot yet be said that the contracts are illegal. It cannot yet be said that media was used as exchange. It cannot yet be said that public property was lost. The file stays open until the acts, maps, decisions and replies are available.
